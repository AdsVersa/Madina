About Us
Family-Owned & Trusted – Over 20 Years of Marine Food & Tech Service

At Madina Ship Supplies, we specialize in foodservice distribution exclusively for the marine industry. With a proven legacy of over two decades, we have built our reputation on strong relationships, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

We continually improve our processes, expand our capabilities, and embrace innovation — ensuring we remain a dependable global partner that ships can trust, no matter the conditions at sea.

Who We Are

Every great journey begins with a single step — and for us, that step was taken with a clear mission: to deliver reliable and innovative food & spares solutions to fleets and maritime businesses worldwide.

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in the marine supply industry, we proudly connect renowned Indian and international brands with vessels across the globe — while ensuring efficient logistics and seamless onboard delivery.

Our success lies not only in the products we supply, but in the lasting relationships we build with ship operators, crew members, business partners, and global procurement teams.
